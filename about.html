Who We Are

Nexzus Engiuneering mega servises is an electrical firm that was created in 10th Novermber, 2020, majored in building/construction of Generating station, transmision injection-stations, distribution sub-station, transmission and distribution lines.

Nexzus Engiuneering mega servises is Africa’s preferred provider of electrical engineering solutions. NEMS provides end-to-end power systems solutions, from developing project concepts through engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, operations and management, At NEMS, we are building on over 5 years’ verifiable experience in generation, transmission and distribution networks to the West African Power Sector. As such, we thrive on building relationships and supporting those relationships with service, quality, innovation and reliability.
